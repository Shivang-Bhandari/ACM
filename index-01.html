About Us.

ACM | Chitkara Univerity

Outstanding ACM Chapter , a student chapter initiated by Chitkara University associated with the ACM society. The Association for Computing Machinery is an international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947 and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society. It is a not-for-profit professional membership group. We organise various events , activities and expert talks so as to help the young minds of our nation to prosper and achieve new hieghts in their career.

EXPERT TALK : MR. SOFI ZAHOOR

Mr. Sofi Zahoor in association with the Association Of Computing Machinery Society of Chitkara University gave a motivational talk to the young aspiring minds of Chitkara University so that they may follow their aims with great determination and focus and achieve great heights and grow on to become benefactors to the society. He explained the Importance of growing into a wise man as well as following your dreams with determination.

WORKSHOP : IOT

ACM Student Chapter (School of Computer Sciences) in association with Netmax Technologies private Ltd. organized a workshop on “Internet of Things” on 11th April,2016 where students participated with full enthusiasm and developed their technical skills. Mr. Jagdeep Singh, from Netmax technologiesPvt Ltd. provided the students with valuable information and insight on IoT. Students learned about the basics of Python and integration of electronic devices using Python

WORKSHOP : PHP

ACM Student Chapter(School of Computer Sciences), Chitkara University organized a Workshop on “PHP Scripting and Database Connectivity” on 14 April 2016 under the guidance of Mr. Naveen Kalra, CEO, SatShri Technologies Pvt. Ltd. Around 60 students attended the workshop with great enthusiasm and zeal. The session started with introduction to various back end languages like Ajax, MySQL and PHP..They created databases and tables using PHP and WAMP server, also connected the HTML page with database using PHP scripts.
